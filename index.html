My name is Sékou-Oumar Kaba, I am a graduate student in machine learning at McGill University and Mila.

I have a background in physics and my research focuses on designing machine learning models that leverage structure and symmetry in data, primarily for applications in physics (with a focus on materials and quantum systems).

My interests also include geometric deep learning, graph representation learning, statistical mechanics and dynamical systems.
